connect+ (WhatsApp CRM)

Flask Ngrok HTML SQLite CSS

Problem Statement:

Sales representatives in the retail and e-commerce industry face a critical challenge: handling 20-40 repetitive WhatsApp inquiries daily ("What is your address?", "What are your operating hours?"). This consumes significant time, diverts attention from high-value sales conversations, and leads to delayed responses that lower customer satisfaction.


Our Solution:

connect+ is an intelligent WhatsApp CRM that automates 40-60% of repetitive customer inquiries, dramatically improving response times to under 1 minute for common questions. The system triages and gathers basic customer information before routing to human sales representatives, freeing up 1-2 hours of daily workload for high-value activities like closing deals.


Key Features:

  • WhatsApp Business API integration for automated messaging
  • AI-powered triage system to filter and categorize incoming inquiries
  • Automated responses for repetitive questions (address, hours, basic product info)
  • Customer conversation history logging and analytics
  • Lead scoring based on initial queries to prioritize high-value prospects
  • Seamless handoff to human sales reps for complex inquiries
  • CRM integration to track and manage customer interactions
  • Secure tunneling with Ngrok for webhook handling
  • SQLite database for storing customer data and conversation logs

Impact:

By automating routine inquiries, sales teams can focus on relationship building and closing deals rather than answering the same questions repeatedly. This leads to improved customer satisfaction through faster responses and increased sales productivity.
